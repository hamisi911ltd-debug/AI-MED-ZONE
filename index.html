AMZ MedAI Academy is more than a learning platform — it is an active research community. Form teams, access clinical datasets, and publish peer-reviewed medical AI research alongside Kenya's top academic hospitals.

Form Research Teams

Create or join research groups of up to 6 clinicians across specialties. Collaborate in real time with shared workspaces, data, and communication tools.

Access Clinical Datasets

Work with fully anonymised, ethics-approved clinical datasets from partner hospitals — including imaging, EHR records, and genomic data — for your research.

Publish & Peer Review

Submit your research for internal peer review by our academic board. Top papers receive support for submission to indexed international medical AI journals.

Mentored by Professors
