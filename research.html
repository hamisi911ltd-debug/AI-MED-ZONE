Research Collaboration Hub

Do Real Medical AI Research Together

AMZ is more than a learning platform — it is an active research community. Starting from Week 10's capstone, learners form teams, access clinical datasets, and publish peer-reviewed medical AI research alongside our academic and clinical partners across East Africa.

Form Research Teams

Create or join research groups of up to 6 clinicians across specialties. Collaborate in real time with shared workspaces, data, and communication tools.

Access Clinical Datasets

Work with fully anonymised, ethics-approved clinical datasets from partner hospitals — including imaging, EHR records, and genomic data — for your research.

Publish & Peer Review

Submit your research for internal peer review by our academic board. Top papers receive support for submission to indexed international medical AI journals.

Mentored by Professors

Research teams are matched with faculty mentors from our academic board to guide methodology and support international publication.

Research Awards & Recognition

Outstanding research earns publication credits, distinction certificates, and recognition by our academic partners.

East Africa Impact Projects

Apply AI research to real clinical challenges in Kenyan healthcare — from malaria detection to maternal health AI — with measurable patient outcome impact.
